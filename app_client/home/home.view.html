Welcome to Washbear eSport portal

This is a demo version of a product concept with limited functionality.
If any defects are found, please let us know.

Currently, you are able to:

  • Create account and login
  • Change your profile details
  • Create / leave a team
  • View teams and other profiles
  • View events

Warning! Please do not use confidential personal information in account registration!
- Team Washbear
